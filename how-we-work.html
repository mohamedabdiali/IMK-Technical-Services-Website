Our Business & Workforce Model

A differentiated approach to manpower solutions

01

Direct Employment & Sponsorship

All workers are direct employees of IMK, ensuring full control, loyalty, and compliance with UAE labour regulations. We maintain full sponsorship of all personnel.

  • Company-sponsored workforce
  • Direct employer responsibility
  • Full visa quota management
  • Employee welfare oversight
02

Integrated On-Site Supervision

Every work team is led by an IMK Site Supervisor/Foreman, ensuring direct management and accountability. Our supervisors act as the single point of contact with main contractors.

  • Dedicated IMK supervisors on site
  • Daily work allocation & productivity control
  • Direct reporting to IMK management
  • Enforcement of HSE and quality standards
03

Dynamic Scalability

We flexibly ramp up or down our on-site workforce based on project phasing and client instructions. Our database of pre-screened workers enables rapid mobilization.

  • Flexible workforce scaling
  • Rapid deployment capability
  • Database of pre-screened workers
  • Project phase-based adjustments
04

End-to-End Administration

IMK manages all visa processing, medicals, insurance, accommodation, and payroll (via WPS) for its employees, providing comprehensive administrative support.

  • Full visa and documentation management
  • Payroll processing through WPS
  • Accommodation and transportation
  • Insurance and statutory compliance

Health, Safety & Environment (HSE)

Safety-first approach embedded in all operations

1

Project-Specific HSE Planning

Development of comprehensive HSE plans tailored to each project's specific requirements and risks.

  • Risk Assessment & Method Statements (RAMS)
  • Emergency Response Plans (ERPs)
  • Site-specific safety protocols
2

Training & Inductions

Mandatory safety training for all personnel before deployment to any project site.

  • Project-specific site inductions
  • Regular toolbox talks
  • HSE awareness sessions
  • Equipment-specific training
3

On-Site Implementation

Daily enforcement of HSE standards with continuous monitoring and improvement.

  • Daily toolbox talks by supervisors
  • Weekly HSE inspections
  • Strict PPE enforcement
  • Hazard reporting system
4

Incident Management

Structured process for incident reporting, investigation, and preventive actions.

  • Immediate incident reporting
  • Root cause analysis
  • Corrective action implementation
  • Lessons learned documentation

Quality Assurance System

Structured quality controls for consistent service delivery

01 Defined Work Procedures

Clear work instructions aligned with project Inspection & Test Plans (ITPs) and client specifications.

02 Layered Inspections

Three-tier checking system: Worker self-check, Foreman verification, and random audits by IMK Quality Officer.

03 Non-Conformance Management

Systematic process to identify, document, and rectify defects promptly with corrective actions.

04 Continuous Improvement

Regular performance monitoring, client feedback analysis, and process optimization.
