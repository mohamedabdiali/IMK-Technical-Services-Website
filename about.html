Company Overview

IMK Technical Services LLC is a Dubai-based technical services and manpower supply company supporting main contractors and developers across the UAE. We specialize in providing compliant, safety-trained, and supervised technical workforce to deliver projects efficiently, safely, and on schedule.

Established in 2025 and headquartered in Dubai, we support the construction, fit-out, maintenance, and infrastructure sectors by supplying project-ready technical manpower under full legal and operational compliance.

Our strength lies in understanding project requirements, mobilizing the right workforce, and maintaining high standards of supervision, safety, and quality on site.

Our Mission & Vision

Driving excellence in technical services and manpower solutions

Our Mission

To be a trusted and reliable partner by delivering safe, compliant, and high-quality technical labour solutions, enabling our clients to execute their projects efficiently and successfully.

We strive to understand project needs and provide reliable workforce solutions that meet the highest standards of safety and quality.

Our Vision

To become the preferred labour supply and technical services subcontractor in the UAE, recognized for operational excellence, unwavering compliance, and a partnership-centric approach.

We aim to set the benchmark for quality and safety in the technical services industry through continuous improvement.
