Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What is your typical response time for manpower requests?

We aim to respond to all manpower requests within 2-4 business hours. For emergency requirements (24-48 hours deployment), we provide immediate acknowledgment and begin mobilization immediately.

Do you provide workers for short-term projects?

Yes, we specialize in both short-term and long-term manpower deployment. We can provide workers for projects ranging from 1 day to several years, with flexible contract terms to suit your project needs.

What types of insurance do you provide for workers?

All our workers are covered by comprehensive insurance policies including Employer's Liability Insurance, Health Insurance, and Workmen's Compensation as per UAE regulations. We also maintain Public Liability Insurance for project sites.

Can you provide specialized skilled workers?

Yes, we maintain a database of skilled workers across various trades including electricians, plumbers, HVAC technicians, steel fixers, carpenters, and more. All workers are verified for their skills and certifications.

What are your working hours for site support?

Our office hours are 10:00 PM to 7:00 AM, Sunday through Thursday. However, we provide 24/7 emergency support for site requirements. Our site supervisors work according to project schedules, including weekends if required.
